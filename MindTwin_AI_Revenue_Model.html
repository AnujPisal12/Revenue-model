Strategic notes

Why this model works

40%

Institutional SaaS is the biggest bet

One school with 1,000 students at ₹100/student/month = ₹12L/year from a single contract. Fewer customers, higher LTV, stickier retention.

Free

B2C freemium drives B2B sales

Students on the free tier become proof points for school deals. A student improving scores with MindTwin is the most effective enterprise sales tool.

OS

EdTech integrations = the real moat

Becoming the orchestration layer on top of PW, Unacademy, and Allen — reading schedules, detecting gaps, personalizing revision — without acquiring their students.

50K+

Marketplace is last-mile monetization

Don't build it before you have 50K+ active students. The 10–20% take rate on tutor / mentor sessions compounds nicely at scale but has near-zero value before then.
